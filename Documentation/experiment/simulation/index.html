Instructions If the highlighted area does not appear immediately on selecting the values, scroll up/down in order to view it. Abbreviations Saturated Water

Tsat = Saturation Temperature (oC)
Psat = Saturation Pressure (kPa)
vf = Specific volume of saturated liquid state (m3/kg)
vg = Specific volume of saturated vapor state (m3/kg)
uf = Specific Internal energy of saturated liquid state (kJ/kg)
ug = Specific Internal energy of saturated vapor state (kJ/kg)
ufg = Change in Internal energy during evaporation (or condensation) (kJ/kg)
hf = Specific Enthalpy of saturated liquid state (kJ/kg)
hg = Specific Enthalpy of saturated vapor state (kJ/kg)
hfg = Change in Enthalpy during evaporation (or condensation) (kJ/kg)
sf = Specific Entropy of saturated liquid state (kJ/kg.K)
sg = Specific Entropy of saturated vapor state (kJ/kg.K)
sfg = Change in Entropy during evaporation (or condensation) (kJ/kg.K)

Superheated Water

v = Specific volume (m3/kg)
u = Specific Internal energy (kJ/kg)
h = Specific Enthalpy (kJ/kg)
s = Specific Entropy (kJ/kg.K)
 Formulae ufg = ug-uf
hfg = hg-hf
sfg = sg-sf
